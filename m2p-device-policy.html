Device Usage Policy

Effective Date: 1 April 2026 Version: 1.0 Audience: All Employees

Confidentiality Notice: No part of this documentation may be reproduced or transmitted in any form or by any means, electronic or mechanical, including photocopying or recording, for any purpose without the express written permission of M2P Solutions Private Limited.

§ 01

Objective

The Company Device Policy has been formulated to introduce an employee-friendly and easy-to-administer scheme that allows employees to obtain any devices they may need in discharging their official duties as employees of M2P Solutions Private Limited and its subsidiaries.

§ 02

Eligibility

  • All employees of the organisation are eligible to participate in this policy, which will be administered as a Company-rented device program.
  • Types of devices: Employees may be provided with mobile phones or smartphones, not exceeding one device per employee, subject to confirmation and approval limits set by the Company.
  • An employee may receive a device valued up to one month of their basic pay, subject to meeting the minimum balance requirement and after accounting for any outstanding dues.
  • Sourcing of any device under this policy requires prior approval from HR and Finance departments.
  • The Company reserves the right to modify, cancel, or replace this policy at any time at its sole discretion.
§ 03

Overview

Under this policy, eligible employees will receive a device that the Company will rent or lease and provide for official use. Employees will have no ownership right over the device during the lease term — it is allotted solely for the discharge of official duties.

§ 04

Procedure

Employees can request a device through the following steps:

1
Access the Tortoise App Log in to the Tortoise platform and browse available devices to raise a request for a device of your choice.
2
No Credentials? Contact HR If you do not have Tortoise app credentials, reach out to the HR team to obtain your login details.
3
Await Confirmation Once your request is submitted, the Company will process the rental or lease arrangement. You will be notified when the request is fulfilled.
§ 05

Sourcing of Devices

  • Employees can place a request for any device on the Tortoise platform, subject to the cost and eligibility criteria defined in this policy.
  • Once a request is placed, the Company will proceed to rent or lease the device for a predetermined period, subject to the rental/lease terms provided by its partners.
  • The Company will pay rentals directly to the renting/leasing partner for the full duration of the term, commencing once the employee's request has been initiated, accepted, and fulfilled.
§ 06

Ownership of the Device

All devices procured under this policy are exclusively owned by the renting/leasing partner and must be returned at the end of the rental/lease term — including upon termination of employment.

  • Devices must be returned to the renting/leasing partner at the end of the lease term or upon termination of employment, whichever occurs first.
  • Employees may directly request the renting/leasing company to transfer device ownership to them; the renting/leasing company may agree at their sole discretion. The Company will not be involved or responsible for this transaction in any manner.
  • Any ownership transfer is subject exclusively to the terms and conditions of the renting/leasing company.
§ 07

Usage of Devices

Every employee availing this policy must ensure the following:

  • Official use only. Devices obtained under this policy must be used primarily for official purposes.
  • Care and condition. Devices must be kept in proper functioning condition. Any damage caused by the employee's actions or negligence will make the employee solely responsible for all related repair costs.
  • Lawful use only. Devices must not be used for any illegal or unlawful activities — by the employee or any third party. The employee bears full responsibility for any consequences arising from such misuse. The Company assumes no liability.
  • Authorised servicing. Any service or repair must be carried out at the employee's expense and only through the authorised service provider as communicated by the renting/leasing company.
§ 08

Miscellaneous

Early Termination

  • If an employee wishes to return a device before the end of the lease term, they may request the Company to place an early termination request with the renting/leasing partner.
  • Any penalties, charges, fees, or dues imposed on the Company due to early termination will be recovered from the employee via payroll adjustment or full and final settlement.

Company Rights

  • The Company reserves the right to physically inspect and verify any device procured under this policy on company premises, at any time and in any manner it deems fit.
  • This policy may be amended or withdrawn at any point at the sole discretion of management.

Employee Responsibilities

  • Employees are personally responsible for the device(s) issued to them — including safety, data stored, protection from viral attacks, internet security, and maintenance throughout the device's lifetime.
  • Failure to comply with any part of this policy may result in disciplinary action, including but not limited to: recovery of dues, repair or servicing costs, legal action, and/or termination of employment.

By availing this policy, employees acknowledge and agree that the Company will proceed to rent/lease devices solely at their request, under terms and conditions set by renting/leasing partners — and that they are bound by all provisions of this document.
