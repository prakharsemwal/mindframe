## Program Structure

CRISIL employees can lease **mobile phones and tablets** on a **6-month** rolling tenure — one of the shorter lease cycles among clients, indicating a preference for flexibility and frequent device refresh.

All devices are leased off the company's books, keeping the program simple from an accounting standpoint.

---

## Employee Experience

### How Ordering Works
- Employees need **manager approval** before a lease is confirmed — there's no automatic approval
- Employees can **choose their own delivery address** (home or office), giving them flexibility
- After delivery, employees must **confirm they've received** the device before the lease officially begins
- **Insurance is mandatory** on every order — employees cannot opt out

### What Employees Can Order
- Mobile phones and tablets only
- Maximum of **1 mobile** and **1 tablet** per employee
- Maximum order value of **₹5,00,000**

---

## Key Features Enabled

- **Salary deduction** — lease payments are deducted directly from payroll
- **Cashify trade-in** — employees can trade in old devices
- **In-app chatbot** — employees have access to a virtual support assistant
- **Tax benefits** — input tax credit is captured on transactions
- **Buyback at end of lease** — devices can be bought back at 2% of original value

## What Makes CRISIL Unique

- **Shorter 6-month lease tenure** — employees refresh devices more frequently than most clients
- **Employee-chosen delivery address** — more flexibility than most clients who restrict to office addresses
- **High-value order cap (₹5L)** — openness to premium devices
